**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Welcome aboard! Here's how to get Jerry The Customer Service Bot running on [Store Name] — takes about 2 minutes:

**Step 1: Install**
Click the install link and authorize Jerry for your Shopify store. One-click OAuth — no code required.

**Step 2: Sync**
Jerry automatically syncs your entire product catalog using semantic embeddings. This usually takes 30-60 seconds depending on catalog size. You'll see a confirmation when it's done.

**Step 3: Go Live**
The Jerry chat widget appears on your storefront automatically. Your customers can start chatting immediately — product search, order tracking, returns, everything works out of the box.

**Your Dashboard:**
You can monitor Jerry's performance anytime — conversations, resolution rate, and attributed revenue are all tracked live.

A few things to know:
- Jerry speaks 50+ languages and auto-detects the customer's language
- Every conversation is protected by WonderwallAi (our AI firewall)
- Jerry escalates to a human when it detects frustration or complex issues

**A note on voice chat:**
Jerry supports voice input and spoken responses. Voice input (speak-to-ask) works best on Chrome for desktop and Android. On iPhones (Safari), Jerry can speak responses back but voice input isn't supported by Apple yet — your customers can always type instead. Voice responses can be toggled on/off by clicking the speaker icon in the chat header.

If you have any questions, just reply to this email. I read every one.

## Prospect Finding

### Where to find Shopify store owners:

**Shopify App Store**
- Search for stores using other chat apps (Tidio, Gorgias, Re:amaze)
- These stores already value customer support automation — Jerry is a direct upgrade

**Shopify Community Forums**
- Store owners posting about support volume, hiring help desk staff, or managing WISMO queries
- Search: "customer support", "chat widget", "support automation"

**LinkedIn**
- Job titles: "Shopify Store Owner", "DTC Brand Founder", "E-commerce Manager"
- Company size: 1-50 employees (sweet spot — big enough to have volume, small enough to need automation)

**Twitter/X**
- Search: "Shopify customer support", "Shopify support tickets", "hiring support agent Shopify"
- Look for store owners complaining about support workload

**Reddit**
- r/shopify, r/ecommerce, r/smallbusiness
- Threads about customer service costs, chatbot recommendations, support scaling
