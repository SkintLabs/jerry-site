## LinkedIn (Professional)

Excited to share something we've been building at Skint Labs.

We recently completed WonderwallAi — an open-source AI firewall SDK that protects LLM applications from prompt injection and data leaks. But that project was born out of a bigger one: Jerry The Customer Service Bot.

Jerry is an AI-powered customer service assistant for Shopify stores. It connects to your store via OAuth, syncs your entire product catalog using semantic embeddings, and handles customer queries 24/7 — product search, order tracking, returns, voice chat in 50+ languages.

The key differentiator: Jerry doesn't do keyword matching. Ask "Do you have something warm for hiking under $100?" and Jerry understands the intent and searches your real catalog semantically. It also tracks revenue attribution — you can see exactly which customer conversations lead to purchases.

When we put Jerry in production, real users immediately started testing the boundaries (prompt injection, system prompt extraction, data exfiltration attempts). That's what led us to build WonderwallAi as a standalone security layer. Now every Jerry conversation is protected by a 4-layer AI firewall.
