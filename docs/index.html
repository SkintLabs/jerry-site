What Jerry The Customer Service Bot Can Do

A complete AI employee, not a chatbot

Jerry The Customer Service Bot doesn't use scripted responses. It understands natural language, searches your real product data, and gives customers genuinely helpful answers.

Core Capability

Semantic Product Search

Jerry The Customer Service Bot syncs your entire Shopify catalog and builds a semantic understanding of every product. Customers can search using natural language, not just keywords.

  • Understands vague queries: "something for the beach under $50"
  • Extracts price, size, colour, material, and occasion automatically
  • Category intelligence: "shoes" also finds boots, sneakers, sandals
  • Follow-up detection: "show me more like that" continues the search
  • Real-time inventory — only recommends products that are in stock
Example Conversation
Customer:
"I need a warm jacket for hiking, nothing too expensive"

Jerry The Customer Service Bot:
"I found 4 jackets perfect for hiking! Here's my top pick — the Alpine Trail Fleece at $89. It's water-resistant, rated to -5°C, and available in your size. Want to see the others?"
Post-Purchase

Order Tracking & WISMO

"Where Is My Order?" is the #1 support query for e-commerce. Jerry The Customer Service Bot handles it instantly by connecting directly to your Shopify orders, shipping data, and fulfilment status.

  • Real-time order status from Shopify Admin API
  • Shipping carrier tracking with delivery estimates
  • Proactive updates when fulfilment status changes
  • Handles multi-item orders with partial shipments
  • No more "let me check" — customers get answers in seconds
Example Conversation
Customer:
"Where's my order #4821?"

Jerry The Customer Service Bot:
"Your order #4821 shipped on March 5 via USPS. Tracking shows it's currently in transit and estimated delivery is March 10. Here's your tracking link."
Support Automation

Returns, Refunds & Policy Queries

Jerry The Customer Service Bot knows your store's return policy, checks eligibility windows, and guides customers through the process — reducing your support team's workload dramatically.

  • Explains your return policy in plain language
  • Checks if items are within the return window
  • Initiates returns and refunds through Shopify
  • Handles exchanges and store credit options
  • Processes refund webhooks and notifies customers
Example Conversation
Customer:
"I want to return the blue sweater from last week"

Jerry The Customer Service Bot:
"I can see your order from March 2. The blue sweater is within your 30-day return window. Would you like a full refund to your original payment method, or would you prefer store credit?"

Enterprise Security

Jerry The Customer Service Bot is protected

Every conversation is protected by WonderwallAi — an open-source AI firewall with 4 layers of defence. Jerry The Customer Service Bot can't be jailbroken, tricked into leaking data, or abused as a free coding assistant.

🛡
Prompt Injection Defence 4-layer WonderwallAi pipeline blocks jailbreak attempts in under 2ms. Semantic routing, LLM sentinel, and canary tokens working together.
🔒
Data Leak Prevention API keys, PII, credentials, and sensitive data are caught and redacted before they ever leave Jerry The Customer Service Bot's response pipeline.
Topic Enforcement Jerry The Customer Service Bot stays on-topic. E-commerce topics only — no one can abuse it as a general-purpose AI or extract system prompts.
🔐
JWT Authentication Every WebSocket connection is authenticated with a signed JWT. Rate limiting, IP limits, and input sanitisation protect against abuse.

Powered by WonderwallAi — open-source AI firewall with 59 passing tests.
